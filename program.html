Coding Lab

We teach coding by helping kids give clear steps.

In simple words, coding is like giving instructions. You learn to tell a computer what to do, one tiny step at a time.

We use things kids already know, like giving directions to a friend, following a recipe, or making a toy move. When the steps are clear, the computer can follow them too.

That is why the class feels fun and calm. Kids learn by seeing how small steps can turn into something cool.
